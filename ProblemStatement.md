# PS2 — AI-Based Governance & Role-Based Access Intelligence Platform

> **Domain:** Agentic AI / Enterprise Governance

---

## Problem Statement

Build an **AI-driven governance platform** that enforces role-based information access within an organization. The system should ensure that AI agents respond to users strictly based on their **Need, Role, and Level (NRL)** while maintaining auditability and compliance.

---

## Key Objectives

- Define organizational roles and permissions (RBAC/NRL)
- Enforce access control for AI-generated responses
- Maintain audit logs of user queries and agent actions
- Prevent unauthorized data exposure by AI agents
